PRIVACY POLICY

This application communicates with the router's admin page and using an http call sequence retrieves the voip settings and displays it to your device.
This app needs to process your router's local ip and the admin's username and password in order to achieve its goal, 
any private details is stored locally and is not transmitted through the internet.

We may keep some telemetry data that WONT include:

- Session cookies
- Username and Password
- VOIP Credentials

Any telemetry data will contain:

- Http request and responses (any data mentioned above will be sencored)
- Your router's version

Also in case of a telemetry in nessesary for your ip to be processed.
Also at any time you may opt out not to send any telemetry data.
